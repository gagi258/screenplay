Fountain cheat sheet

Scene heading
Start a line with INT. / EXT. (works in Danish too). Or force any line with a leading dot: .A STRANGE PLACE. Leading scene numbers like 1. are fine.
Action
Just write normally. Any paragraph that isn't another element is action.
Character
A name in CAPS on its own line, with a blank line above. Force with @: @McKenzie.
Dialogue
Lines directly under a character.
Parenthetical
A line in (parentheses) under the character.
Dual dialogue
Add ^ after the second character's name to place two speeches side by side.
Transition
An ALL-CAPS line ending in TO:, or force with >. Centered text: >THE END<.
Emphasis
*italics*, **bold**, ***bold italics***, _underline_.
Title page
At the very top: Title:, Credit:, Author:, Draft date: — then a blank line.
Other
=== forces a page break. # Section and = synopsis and [[notes]] are for you only and never print.
Danish & bilingual
æ/ø/å are fully supported. Type i./e. for INT./EXT. and d./n. for the day/night word in your primary language — these are built in to every project. Add your own under ⚙︎ Shortcuts.
